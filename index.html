The Future Starts Here

23 May 2018

My first impression as I entered the V&A’s new exhibition, ‘The Future Starts Here’, was of a giant playroom for grown-ups. It is spread across one large space lit like a bowling alley, with the exhibits of the first section housed in fluorescent-coloured plastic structures. A huge drone is suspended from the ceiling; the largest object ever exhibited at the V&A, it has the wingspan of a Boeing 747.

I felt unsettled in so many ways.
