Yugen - The Royal Ballet

26 March 2018

Leonard Bernstein’s ‘Chichester Psalms’ are by turns rousing, light, angular, playful and exquisite. I could use exactly those words to describe the choreography of Wayne McGregor’s new ballet, Yugen, which premiered at the Royal Opera House earlier this month. I thought it was fresh, spare and the perfect response to this extraordinary piece of music.
