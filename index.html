The Favourite

13 January 2019

It feels rare in our world of social media, live news, and constant information supply to be surprised by anything and perhaps even rarer to be able to approach something with few pre-conceived ideas. Yet this was my experience watching The Favourite last Saturday. It was completely refreshing to walk out of the cinema and still be processing what I had seen. In fact, I couldn’t voice an articulate opinion on it until we were almost halfway home.
