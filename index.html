Modigliani

17 January 2018

The faces and bodies of many different people currently fill several galleries at the Tate Modern. But with a first glance around me, all I could see was one man: Modigliani. This impression echoes the remark made by the subject of one of the portraits, Jean Cocteau. It is quoted in the show and many other places besides: ‘It doesn’t look like me. But it does look like Modigliani, which is better.’
