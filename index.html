Rodin and the Art of Ancient Greece

17 July 2018

This exhibition is one of those that’s been on for ages and which I’d been meaning to see, finally managing to get there a few weeks before it closes later this month. We went on a sweltering Saturday morning in the middle of a miraculously long, hot summer, and it felt like entering a sanctuary. A beautiful, big, cool gallery in the heart of the otherwise buzzing British Museum, sectioned off but still very open, the amazing volume of the space emphasised.
