Impressionists in London

11 February 2018

The purpose of this exhibition, as explained in the first room, is to display the work of artists exiled to London from Paris and France during the Franco-Prussian war and ensuing Paris Commune. According to its title, you might reasonably expect an Impressionist exhibition but Impressionism being on the rise during the same time period seems to be purely coincidental. You may feel a premonition that what is inside is not exactly what it says on the tin.
